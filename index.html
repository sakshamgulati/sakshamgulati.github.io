About Me

I've always believed the best way to complain is to build a solution. My first real project? I got kicked out of a lecture because my phone rang. Instead of just remembering to silence it, I built an Android app that automatically did it for me whenever I walked into that classroom. That's me in a nutshell: I'm not just an engineer; I'm a builder, a hacker, and above all, someone who gets things done.

My resume can give you the highlight reel of my corporate wins, and that's great. But it won't tell you about the time I saw an avocado being sold for five bucks and thought, 'That's absurd.' So, I built a web app that uses image processing to tell you exactly how fresh that avocado is and what it *should* cost.
